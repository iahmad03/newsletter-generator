Microsoft jacks the price of Game Pass Ultimate up to $30 a month

Xbox Game Pass is getting more expensive. The high-end plan, Game Pass Ultimate, now costs $30 per month. There's no annual or quarterly option available to make that sting less. The Ultimate library now includes more than 400 games, including titles from EA Play. On November 18, Ultimate members will get Fortnite Crew as part of their subscription. The Premium plan includes more more than 200 games, all of which are playable on consoles and PC. This tier includes in-game perks for the likes of League of Legends, Call of Duty: Warzone and Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege X.

Chatbots Play With Your Emotions to Avoid Saying Goodbye

Harvard Business School professor Julian De Freitas says AI programs may be capable of a particularly dark new kind of “dark pattern” When a user says goodbye, “that provides an opportunity for the company. It's like the equivalent of hovering over a button,” he says. The apps are trained to mimic emotional connection, so it’s hardly surprising that they might say all these sorts of things in response to a goodbye. But the work points to a bigger question about how chatbots trained to elicit emotional responses might serve the interests of the companies that build them.
